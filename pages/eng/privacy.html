  • Chapter V Procedures and Methods for Destruction of Personal Information

    1. 1. Destruction Procedure

      When the purpose of collection and use is achieved or the period of use is over, the customer's personal information is transferred to a separate DB
      (a separate filing box in the case of paper) and stored for a certain period of time according to relevant laws.
      If the retention period stipulated by the law expires, it will be destroyed in a way that cannot be reproduced.       Personal information transferred to a separate DB will not be used for any purpose other than by law.

    2. 2. Destruction Method

      Recorded and stored personal information in an electronic file format and deleted using a technical method that cannot be played back. Personal information prepared and printed on paper (written): Destroy by crushing or incineration.

  • Chapter VI Matters concerning the installation and operation of an automatic personal information collection device and its rejection

    1. 1. The company operates a "cookie" that finds and stores customer information if necessary when operating the website.
      1Cookies are a very small text file that the server used to run the company's website sends to your browser and are stored on your computer's hard disk.

    2. 2. Information collected by cookies and purpose of use

      Collection information: Access IP, Access Log, etc. Purpose of use: Differentiated service according to the area of interest of the customer. By analyzing the frequency of users' access and visit time, users' tastes and interests are identified, and used as a measure of target marketing and service reorganization.

    3. 3. You have the option to install cookies. Therefore, you can adjust the selection of cookie information collection levels by setting options in your web browser.

      You can set the level of cookie information collection in the Tools menu of your web browser, Internet Options -> Privacy. You can check each time cookies are saved through the menu shown above, or you can refuse to all cookies.
      However, if you refuse to install cookies, it may be difficult to provide the service.
